Themes, Featues and Hosting Link

Theme

White was used across the website to create a minimalistic tone while also making the page appear extremely spacious. A soft red, rather than a vibrant red, was chosen on the website to generate a sense of warmth and approachability for users while they browsed the website. This eventually achieves the goal of attempting to express to the user a sense of warmth and tranquility.The hues were chosen to produce an attractive and sophisticated appearance. The hues also balanced each other out so that the designs throughout the website could come together and remind the user that they were still on the same website.
